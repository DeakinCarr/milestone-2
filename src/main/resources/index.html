About Covid-19

Coronavirus disease (COVID-19)

Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by a newly discovered coronavirus. Most people infected with the COVID-19 will experience mild to moderate respiratory illness and recover without requiring special treatment. Older people, and those with underlying medical problems like cardiovascular disease, diabetes, chronic respiratory disease, and cancer are more likely to develop serious illness.

The best way to prevent and slow down transmission is be well informed about the COVID-19 virus, the disease it causes and how it spreads. Protect yourself & others from infection by washing your hands or using an alcohol based rub frequently and not touching your face.

COVID-19 Transmission

How Coronavirus Spreads

People can catch COVID-19 from others who have the virus. The disease can spread from person to person through small droplets from the nose or mouth which are spread when a person with COVID-19 coughs or exhales.

COVID-19 symptoms

Coronavirus COVID-19 Symptoms

The most common symptoms of COVID-19 are fever, tiredness, and dry cough. Some patients may have aches and pains, nasal congestion, runny nose, sore throat or diarrhea.

High Fever

According to the WHO, the most common symptom of Covid-19 is a fever. Fever is a key symptom, experts say.

Sore Throat

A scratchy sensation in the throat, pain in the throat area that becomes worse when swallowing or talking.

Dry Cough

Basically, a dry cough is "one where no mucus or phlegm is produced with the cough" and "[one] that doesn’t go away."

Sneezing

Sneezing is very common symptom in Coronavirus patients. Sneezing is the act of expelling an involuntary expulsion of air from the nose and mouth.

Shortness of Breath

Commonly known as tightening in the chest, air hunger, difficulty breathing, feeling of suffocation.

Vomiting

It’s not one of the regular symptoms of coronavirus, but has been found to occur in some patients.

COVID-19 Prevention

Coronavirus COVID-19 Prevention

You can protect yourself and help prevent spreading the coronavirus (COVID-19) to others if you do all the step shown in this infographics. You can stay safe from coronavirus and stop the spread if you are affected

Wash Your Hands

Wash your hands frequently and regularly with an alcohol based hand rub or wash them with soap and water.

Wear Face Mask

Wear a surgical mask and avoid touching your face. Hands touch many surfaces and can pick up viruses once contaminated.

Avoid Close Contact

Please maintain at least a 1.5 metre distance between yourself and anyone around you.

Stay at Home

Stay home, stay safe. If you have a fever, cough or difficulty breathing, go get tested.

Use Soap or Sanitizer

Use alcohol based hand sanitizer or soap when washing your hands. You should wash your hands for around the same time as it takes to hum the "Happy Birthday" song twice.

Keep Doctors Informed

If you are think you are suffering with any of the above symptoms. Please call a doctor as soon as possible for medical advice.
