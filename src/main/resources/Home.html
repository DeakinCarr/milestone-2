What can I do here?

On the COVIDDB website you are able to find data related to infection and death rates across the globe. The information is displayed on both a global and regional scale with up to date and easy to find results.

Click here to learn more

Who is this for?

The COVIDDB website is designed for a wide user group looking to further their own knowledge about the ongoing pandemic. Primarily it is designed with needs of three main user groups in mind.

  1. People looking for quick up to date facts about the ongoing pandemic
  2. People looking for information in different countries or regions
  3. Officials looking to inform themselves deeper on the overall and longterm impacts of the pandemic
